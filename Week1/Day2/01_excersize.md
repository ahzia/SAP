### Step 2: Simple Explanation

**Cloud Computing = Using the Internet to Store and Access Things**

Instead of keeping files on your computer, you keep them on the internet where you can access them from anywhere!

**Definition:** Cloud computing refers to the delivery of computing services over the internet, including storage, processing power, and software applications.
