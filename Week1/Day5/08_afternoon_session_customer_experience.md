### What is User Experience (UX)?

**Definition:** User experience (UX) is how a person feels when interacting with a product, system, or service.

**In Simple Terms:** How easy, pleasant, and effective something is to use.

**Key Elements:**
- **Usability** - Is it easy to use?
- **Accessibility** - Can everyone use it?
- **Efficiency** - Does it save time?
- **Satisfaction** - Does it make you happy?

**Think of it as:** The difference between a smooth, pleasant journey and a frustrating, confusing one.

---

### What is Customer Experience (CX)?

**Definition:** Customer experience is the overall experience a customer has with a company across all touchpoints.

**In Simple Terms:** How customers feel about their entire journey with a company.

**Includes:**
- Website experience
- Product experience
- Service experience
- Support experience
- Overall satisfaction
